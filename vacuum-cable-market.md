<p><ul><li>High Vacuum</li><li>UHV</li><li>XHV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum cable market is segmented into three main types based on pressure levels: High Vacuum, Ultra-High Vacuum (UHV), and Extremely High Vacuum (XHV). High Vacuum cables operate at pressures between 10^-3 to 10^-7 Torr, suitable for many industrial applications. UHV cables function in the range of 10^-7 to 10^-9 Torr, used in advanced scientific experiments and particle accelerators. XHV cables work below 10^-9 Torr, essential for cutting-edge research in quantum physics and materials science, requiring rigorous contamination control.</p></p>
