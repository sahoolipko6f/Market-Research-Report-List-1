<p><p>The Open-Loop Hall-Effect Current Sensor market is categorized into Linear Output and Threshold Output types. Linear Output sensors provide a continuous voltage signal proportional to the measured current, allowing for precise monitoring and control in applications like motor drives and power management. In contrast, Threshold Output sensors activate when the current surpasses a predefined level, ideal for overcurrent protection and fault detection. Both types serve distinct purposes, catering to different needs in industrial and consumer electronics.</p></p>
