<p><p>The smokeless gunpowder market is categorized into three main types: single base, double base, and three base gunpowder. Single base gunpowder typically consists of nitrocellulose, offering reduced smoke and minimal residue. Double base gunpowder combines nitrocellulose and nitroglycerin, enhancing energy and combustion efficiency. Three base gunpowder incorporates additional compounds such as nitroguanidine for improved stability and performance. These classifications influence applications in military, industrial, and sporting sectors, each offering distinct advantages in terms of performance and environmental impact.</p></p>
