<p><p>The hydraulic forging press market comprises several key types, including closed die forging, open die forging, extrusion presses, and other specialized methods. Closed die forging involves shaping metal within two die halves, ensuring precision and complex designs. Open die forging permits greater flexibility, allowing metal to be shaped between flat or contoured surfaces. Extrusion presses force materials through a die to create elongated shapes. Other market segments include hybrid processes and specialized tools catering to unique forging requirements, enhancing versatility in manufacturing.</p></p>
