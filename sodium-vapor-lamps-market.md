<p><p>Sodium vapor lamps are categorized into two main types: high-pressure sodium (HPS) lamps and low-pressure sodium (LPS) lamps. HPS lamps produce bright, yellowish light and are commonly used for street lighting and industrial applications due to their high efficiency and long lifespan. In contrast, LPS lamps emit a distinct monochromatic yellow light, often utilized for outdoor lighting and security applications. Both types are valued for energy efficiency, longevity, and effectiveness in illuminating large areas, contributing significantly to the lighting market.</p></p>
