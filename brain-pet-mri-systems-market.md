<p><ul><li>Traditional PET-MRI Systems</li><li>Helium-free PET-MRI Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Brain PET-MRI systems market comprises two main types: Traditional PET-MRI Systems and Helium-free PET-MRI Systems. Traditional systems combine positron emission tomography (PET) and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) using helium-cooled magnets, offering high-resolution imaging for brain studies. Helium-free systems utilize alternative cooling methods, eliminating dependency on helium, which addresses supply issues and environmental concerns. Both types enhance diagnostic capabilities in neurology, but helium-free systems represent a more sustainable approach, driving advancements in medical imaging technology.</p></p>
