<p><p>The Electromagnetic Position Tracking System market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless. Wired systems utilize physical connections, such as cables, to transmit signals between tracking devices and controllers, ensuring stable and accurate positioning but limiting mobility. Conversely, wireless systems employ radio frequency or infrared signals, offering greater flexibility and ease of movement, making them ideal for applications in dynamic environments. Each type serves distinct use cases, influencing preferences based on accuracy, convenience, and application requirements.</p></p>
