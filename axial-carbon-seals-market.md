<p><p>The axial carbon seals market comprises two main types: lip seals and combined seals. Lip seals are designed to prevent leakage by creating a tight barrier between rotating and stationary components, typically effective in moderate temperature and pressure conditions. Combined seals integrate features of both lip seals and other sealing technologies, providing enhanced performance in challenging environments. These seals find applications in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and industrial machinery, where reliable sealing solutions are crucial for operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
