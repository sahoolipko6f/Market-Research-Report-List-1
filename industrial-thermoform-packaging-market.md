<p><p>The industrial thermoform packaging market encompasses various types, including clamshell, blister, and skin packaging, along with others like windowed packaging and tubs & cups. Clamshell packaging features two hinged halves, providing visibility and protection. Blister packaging is characterized by a rigid plastic shell with a backing, ideal for encapsulating small items. Skin packaging involves a product sealed against a backing with a thin film, enhancing presentation. Windowed packaging allows consumers to view contents, while tubs and cups offer versatile storage solutions.</p></p>
