<p><p>The polyester resins market primarily comprises unsaturated and saturated polyester resins. Unsaturated polyester resins (UPRs) are widely used in applications such as automotive parts and fiberglass products due to their excellent mechanical properties and versatility. They cure with reactive diluents, allowing for easy processing. Saturated polyester resins, on the other hand, are used in coatings and adhesives, offering better chemical resistance and durability. Both types cater to diverse industries, driving innovation and demand for high-performance materials.</p></p>
