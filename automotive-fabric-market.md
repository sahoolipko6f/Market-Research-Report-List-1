<p><ul><li>Woven</li><li>Nonwoven</li><li>Composites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive fabric market comprises three main types: woven, nonwoven, and composites. Woven fabrics are created by interlacing threads, offering durability and aesthetic appeal, commonly used in upholstery and interiors. Nonwoven fabrics are formed through bonding materials without weaving, providing lightweight and cost-effective solutions, ideal for insulation and sound absorption. Composites combine various materials to enhance strength and performance, suitable for high-demand applications like seating and structural components, driving innovation and efficiency in automotive design.</p></p>
