<p><ul><li>Body Acoustic Insulation</li><li>Engine Acoustic Insulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive acoustic insulation materials market is segmented into body acoustic insulation and engine acoustic insulation. Body acoustic insulation focuses on reducing external noise and vibrations in the vehicle cabin, enhancing passenger comfort. It typically includes materials like foam, mats, and barriers applied to doors, floors, and roofs. Engine acoustic insulation, on the other hand, targets noise from the engine compartment, minimizing sound transmission to the cabin. This includes heat-resistant materials that effectively absorb and dampen engine noise, contributing to a quieter driving experience.</p></p>
