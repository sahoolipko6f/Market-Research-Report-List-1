<p><p>The in-line valves market includes three main types: manual valves, pneumatic valves, and electromagnetic valves. Manual valves rely on physical operation for control, offering simplicity and reliability in applications. Pneumatic valves utilize compressed air for actuation, providing fast and efficient operation ideal for automated systems. Electromagnetic valves are electrically controlled, enabling precise regulation of fluid flow, often used in automated and remote applications where quick response is essential. Each type serves distinct needs across various industries.</p></p>
