<p><strong>The Cloud Video Conferencing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SVC Type</li><li>AVC Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cloud Video Conferencing market consists of two primary types: SVC (Scalable Video Coding) and AVC (Advanced Video Coding). SVC allows for adaptable video quality based on network conditions, offering a seamless experience across varying bandwidths. It supports multiple resolutions and bitrates, making it suitable for diverse environments. Conversely, AVC is a more traditional encoding method focusing on efficient compression and high-quality video. While AVC can deliver superior video quality, it may not be as flexible in fluctuating network situations as SVC.</p></p>
