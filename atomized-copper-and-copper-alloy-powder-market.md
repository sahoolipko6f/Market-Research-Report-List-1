<p><p>The Atomized Copper and Copper Alloy Powder Market is categorized into two main types: water atomized and air atomized powders. Water atomized powders are produced by rapidly cooling molten metal with water, resulting in finer particle sizes and a higher level of cleanliness, ideal for applications like additive manufacturing. In contrast, air atomized powders are created using air jets which yield larger particles and are typically used in applications such as metal injection molding and powder metallurgy processes.</p></p>
