<p><p>The operating bed market is segmented into two main types: motorized and non-motorized beds. Motorized operating beds are equipped with electric or hydraulic systems, allowing for adjustable height and positioning, enhancing surgeon convenience and patient access. Both types cater to various healthcare settings, balancing comfort, functionality, and budget, with motorized beds typically preferred in advanced surgical environments.</p></p>
