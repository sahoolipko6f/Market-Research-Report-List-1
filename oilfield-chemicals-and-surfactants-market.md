<p><p>The oilfield chemicals and surfactants market comprises various types based on their ionic nature. Non-ionic surfactants are neutral and effective in varying conditions, while cationic surfactants, with a positive charge, improve emulsification and dispersion. Amphoteric surfactants can act as both anionic and cationic, offering versatility. Other types include specialty chemicals tailored for specific applications, all aimed at optimizing oil extraction and production processes.</p></p>
