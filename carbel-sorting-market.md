<p><p>Carbel Sorting encompasses two primary market types: Linear Carbel Sorting and Ring Carbel Sorting. Linear Carbel Sorting operates along a straight path, efficiently organizing items based on specific criteria, making it ideal for high-volume, streamlined operations. In contrast, Ring Carbel Sorting utilizes a circular design, allowing for continuous movement and higher sorting capacity, particularly suited for complex or diverse product lines. Both types enhance efficiency and accuracy in sorting processes across various industries, catering to different operational needs.</p></p>
