<p><p>The silicone hygienic diaphragm valve market features two primary types: weir type and weirless radial diaphragm type. The weir type valve utilizes a raised seating area to guide flow, providing effective sealing and control over medium. In contrast, the weirless radial diaphragm type eliminates the raised seat, allowing for smoother flow and easier cleaning, making it ideal for applications requiring high hygiene standards. Both types are essential in industries like pharmaceuticals and food processing, ensuring product safety and compliance.</p></p>
