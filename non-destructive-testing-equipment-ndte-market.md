<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic testing equipment</li><li>Magnetic particle testing equipment</li><li>Visual inspection equipment</li><li>Radiography testing equipment</li><li>Penetrant testing equipment</li><li>Eddy current testing equipment</li><li>Acoustic emission testing equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing Equipment (NDTE) encompasses a range of technologies used to evaluate the integrity of materials without causing damage. Ultrasonic testing equipment utilizes sound waves to detect imperfections, while magnetic particle testing equipment reveals surface defects using magnetic fields. Visual inspection equipment offers direct observation for flaws. Radiography testing employs radiation for internal structure examination, and penetrant testing identifies surface cracks with colored dyes. Eddy current testing detects conductive material flaws via electromagnetic induction, and acoustic emission testing monitors stress and flaws through acoustic signals.</p></p>
