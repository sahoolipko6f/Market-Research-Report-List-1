<p><p>The flat panel display market comprises several key technologies: Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED), Liquid Crystal Display (LCD), and Plasma Display (PDP). OLED offers vibrant colors and deep blacks with flexibility, making it ideal for thin screens. LCD, utilizing liquid crystals, is prevalent for its efficiency and affordability, commonly found in televisions and monitors. PDP, once popular for large screens, delivers excellent color and contrast but has largely been surpassed by LCD and OLED due to energy efficiency and bulkiness concerns.</p></p>
