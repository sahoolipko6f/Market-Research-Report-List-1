<p><strong>The Silencer Elbow Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Curved</li><li>Right Angle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silencer Elbow Market is segmented into two main types: Curved and Right Angle elbows. Curved elbows are designed to provide a gradual change in direction, minimizing turbulence and pressure loss in the airflow, making them suitable for applications requiring smooth transitions. In contrast, Right Angle elbows feature a sharp 90-degree turn, which can induce more turbulence and noise but are often more space-efficient. Each type serves specific needs in industries where efficient airflow management is crucial.</p></p>
