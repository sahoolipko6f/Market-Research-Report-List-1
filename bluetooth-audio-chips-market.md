<p><strong>The Bluetooth Audio Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ordinary Chip</li><li>SoC Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bluetooth audio chips market is categorized into ordinary chips and System-on-Chip (SoC) solutions. Ordinary chips typically provide basic Bluetooth functionality, focusing on audio transmission with limited integration, often requiring additional components for complete operation. In contrast, SoC chips integrate multiple functionalities, including audio processing, Bluetooth connectivity, and sometimes even power management, into a single unit. This integration leads to reduced size, lower power consumption, and cost savings, making SoC solutions increasingly popular for modern audio devices.</p></p>
