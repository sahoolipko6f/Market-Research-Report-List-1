<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Extender Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Fiber Type</li><li>Multimode Fiber Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber extender market is primarily segmented into two types: Single Mode Fiber (SMF) and Multimode Fiber (MMF). Single Mode Fiber is designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a single light wave to minimize signal loss and maximize bandwidth, making it ideal for telecom and data center applications. Conversely, Multimode Fiber supports multiple light modes, suitable for shorter distances, often used in LANs and data communication within buildings. Each type caters to specific connectivity needs and bandwidth requirements in various industries.</p></p>
