<p><p>The Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser market is segmented into Single Mode and Multi-Mode categories. Single Mode lasers utilize a single light path, offering high precision and excellent beam quality, making them ideal for applications requiring detailed work, like micro-machining and telecommunications. In contrast, Multi-Mode lasers support multiple light paths, providing higher power output and efficiency, suitable for applications such as material processing and marking. Each type serves distinct industry needs, reflecting varied requirements in performance and application.</p></p>
