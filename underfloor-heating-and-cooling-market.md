The electric system utilizes electric heating cables or mats to warm the floor surface, ideal for smaller areas or retrofits. In contrast, the hydronic system circulates warm water through pipes installed beneath the floor, suitable for larger spaces and offering efficient heating. Both systems enhance comfort and can be integrated with smart home technology for better energy management.</p></p>
