<p><p>The semiconductor market distinguishes between UV and non-UV tapes based on their adhesive properties and applications. UV tapes are sensitive to ultraviolet light, allowing for easy removal and repositioning during the manufacturing process, making them ideal for photolithography and temporary bonding. Non-UV tapes, on the other hand, are designed for stronger, permanent adhesion in various applications, including die attachment and surface protection. Both types play critical roles in enhancing production efficiency and ensuring device reliability in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
