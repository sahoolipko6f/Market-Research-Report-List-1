<p><p>The ocean energy power station market comprises various technologies harnessing oceanic energies. Tidal power stations utilize the gravitational pull of the moon and sun to generate energy from tidal movements. Ocean temperature difference power stations exploit the temperature disparity between surface and deep-sea water for energy conversion. Wave energy stations capture energy from surface waves. Ocean current power stations harness kinetic energy from oceanic currents. Seawater salt concentration difference stations use salinity gradients to produce electricity, tapping into the chemical energy of seawater.</p></p>
