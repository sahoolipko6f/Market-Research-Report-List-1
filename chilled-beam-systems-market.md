<p><strong>The Chilled Beam Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Chilled Beams</li><li>Passive Chilled Beams</li><li>Multiservice Chilled Beams</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chilled Beam Systems are advanced HVAC solutions designed for energy-efficient temperature control. Active chilled beams utilize both convection and forced airflow, integrating chilled water and ventilation, making them suitable for spaces needing precise climate control. Passive chilled beams rely solely on convection and do not use mechanical fans, making them quieter and more energy-efficient for areas with lower heating and cooling demands. Multiservice chilled beams combine cooling, heating, and ventilation in one system, providing comprehensive climate management while saving space and energy.</p></p>
