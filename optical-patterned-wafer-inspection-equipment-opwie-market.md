<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Optical Patterned Wafer Inspection Equipment (OPWIE) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bright-Field Inspection Systems</li><li>Dark-Field Inspection Systems</li><li>Electron Beam Inspection Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Patterned Wafer Inspection Equipment (OPWIE) refers to systems used to detect defects in semiconductor wafers during manufacturing. Bright-field inspection systems utilize transmitted light to highlight defects against a uniform background, ideal for surface defects. Dark-field inspection systems, on the other hand, illuminate the surface at an angle, making defects visible against a dark background, effective for identifying particles and irregularities. Electron beam inspection systems use a focused electron beam to provide high-resolution imaging of wafer structures, enabling precise defect characterization.</p></p>
