<p><p>The EPDM sealing products market is categorized into two primary types: sulfonation and thermoplasticity. Sulfonation involves chemically treating EPDM to enhance its weather resistance and durability, making it ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, thermoplastic EPDM offers flexibility and processability, allowing it to be molded and reshaped easily, which is advantageous for a variety of applications. Both types serve distinct needs in industries such as automotive, construction, and consumer goods, addressing different performance and adaptability requirements.</p></p>
