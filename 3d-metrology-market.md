<p><p>The 3D Metrology market encompasses various technologies for precise measurement and inspection. Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMM) utilize tactile or optical sensors for accuracy in dimensional checks. Optical Digitizers and Scanners (ODS) focus on capturing the geometry of objects through laser or structured light techniques, ideal for complex surfaces. Vision Measurement Machines (VMM) employ camera systems to evaluate part features for quality control, integrating image processing to enhance measurement efficiency. Together, these technologies ensure high precision in manufacturing and engineering applications.</p></p>
