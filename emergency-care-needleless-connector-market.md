<p><strong>The Emergency Care Needleless Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Fluid Displacement</li><li>Negative Fluid Displacement</li><li>Neutral Displacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Emergency Care Needleless Connector market is categorized into three types based on fluid displacement mechanisms: Positive Fluid Displacement, Negative Fluid Displacement, and Neutral Displacement. </p><p>Positive Fluid Displacement connectors push fluid out when the catheter is accessed, minimizing blood reflux. Negative Fluid Displacement connectors pull fluid back, which may lead to some reflux potential. Neutral Displacement connectors eliminate reflux during use while maintaining consistent fluid flow. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, enhancing safety and efficiency in emergency care settings.</p></p>
