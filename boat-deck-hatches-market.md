<p><strong>The Boat Deck Hatches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Watertight</li><li>Non-Watertight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boat deck hatches are crucial components for marine vessels, designed to provide access while ensuring safety. The market is divided into two main types: watertight and non-watertight hatches. Watertight hatches are engineered to prevent water ingress, making them essential for maintaining vessel integrity in rough conditions. Non-watertight hatches, on the other hand, offer easier access for general use and maintenance but are not designed to withstand water pressure. Each type serves specific needs based on vessel use and operational environment.</p></p>
