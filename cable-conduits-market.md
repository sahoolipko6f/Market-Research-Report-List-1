<p><ul><li>Rigid Cable Conduit</li><li>Flexible Cable Conduit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cable conduits market is primarily divided into rigid and flexible cable conduits. Rigid cable conduits are made from hard materials like PVC or metal and provide robust protection for electrical wiring in fixed installations, making them suitable for industrial and commercial applications. In contrast, flexible cable conduits are made from softer materials, allowing for easier installation in tight spaces and adaptability to movements. They are ideal for dynamic environments where flexibility and mobility are crucial, like in manufacturing or automotive sectors.</p></p>
